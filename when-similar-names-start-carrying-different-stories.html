The psychology of the almost-same

But when they appear similar, curiosity grows. The mind leans in. People start asking more layered questions, sometimes not out of confusion but out of responsibility.

In a clinic, that instinct makes sense. Similar names invite closer reading. They encourage people to notice packaging, phrasing, habit, and internal workflow.
